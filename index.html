My name is Andrea Martz

Andrea Martz is a rising self-taught web developer and jack-of-all-trades in professional certification testing. She has worked 100% remotely for most of her career, while working 100% onsite as a mom to two incredible boys. As the only employee at a small company, Andrea improved products and processes to reduce costs and enhance client engagement and satisfaction. By necessity, she also was the Quality Assurance person checking her own work…and no errors ever went to a client. Andrea is happiest when there is variety in her work, interesting problems to solve, and the chance to collaborate, because that almost always means she’ll get to learn new skills and help others learn also.

In her personal life, she is a known finder of resources for parents who are looking for ways to advocate for their highly gifted kids in K-12 schools. She also enjoys exploring national parks and taking walks in the neighborhood with her husband.

Currently, Andrea is on a quest without end to build her web development skills. Her goal is to become a full-stack web developer working to do good in the world with a team of friendly problem-solvers like herself.
